LIVPOL

Privacy-preserving crowd estimation · UNIGE

An optical-encoding pipeline that separates crowd-density estimation from facial identity. Frequency-domain analysis shows density stays recoverable while identifying detail is suppressed by design — a privacy guarantee enforced at the optical layer, not bolted on after.
